For the rest of this first section, let's talk about the Spatial One brief. But first, let's put you into groups. I think 6 groups of 3 people?
The brief is all around care. You need to make a caring app. But caring for who? How? Could it be around sharing important information? Which communities do you already care about or for? Why is it important to share this information spatially?
To be clear, I'm not expecting you to make a complete app - that's for the second half of the year, launching at least on TestFlight. The aim is to work like Apple works, building the prototype of an app in a group, with as much physical and code experiments as possible - not presentations, rather demonstrations and then discussion about the actual thing, not "the vision".
You are going to build the prototypes for your app in paper (more about this next week) and in code using RealityKit and Reality Composer Pro. Lets explore those links together.
