Next lecture we are going to start with everyone showing their 1 minute presentations on which ARKit anchor they are going to concentrate on for the rest of the year, how it fits into their practice and their initial thought. Have a look at the ARKit in visionOS homepage for a complete list. TLDR: you must choose one of:
  • Hand anchors (hands will be the interaction method for all apps, but you want to augment hands specifically)
  • Plane anchors - building an experience that works with any floor, table or wall (pick vertical or horizontal)
  • Image anchors - tracking flat images
  • Object anchors - tracking 3D objects
  • World anchors - being able to return to the same space and see the same virtual objects
